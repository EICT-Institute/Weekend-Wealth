Our Mission
"Our mission is the dedication to the education of children, by making sure no child is left behind due to conflict or any future pendemic. We encourage to students to use the means available on EDU Weekend which enable a strong academic platform where students are encouraged to achieve the highest academic standard. When we work together we can promote and develop a learning environment where students can reach their full potential, by participating in all EDU Wealth programmes. We encourage volunteers to contribute to the future of Africa, by believing in each child who are unique and are gifted special talents which EDU Wealth gives a platform for children to shine This can only be acheived by motivation, encouragement, and hard work, children will be able to reach great heights. We believe that there are no secrets to success. It is the result of preparation, hard work and learning from failure. Education is what remains after one has forgotten what one has learned in school."
